How to get a Kagune?

Kagune Spins

Kagunes are obtained through Kagune Spins that cost 150 Chikaras each. You can purchase them in the store or obtain them from codes on the official Discord.

📍 Where to find the Kagune Spin

Kagunes can be obtained from the red capsule thing next to the Ghoul NPC. This can be found next to the parking lot behind the Gym training zone.

Kagune Mastery

To unlock all abilities of your Kagune, you must level up its mastery by defeating bosses, NPCs, and completing quests.
