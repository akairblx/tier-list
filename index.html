How to get a devil fruit?

Fruits under the trees

Fruits spawn under the trees throughout the server at 45 min when a server starts for the first time, and then every 30 min after. Fruits despawn in 10 min.

NEW: Now the spawn timer starts when the fruit is picked up or despawned.

Other ways to get fruits

Another way to get fruits is by using fruit spins that are obtained from codes given by the game creators on the official Discord, and directly in the store buying random fruits.

NEW: Phoenix can be obtained through the Game Pass.
NEW: Barrier can be obtained through the Battle Pass.

Reminder

Fruit Mastery

To unlock all abilities of the fruits, you must level up its mastery by doing farming, killing bosses, NPCs among others.
